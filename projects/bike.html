Multi-Load Structural Analysis of Bicycle Frame

Objective

To evaluate structural performance of a bicycle frame under multiple realistic loading conditions and compare candidate materials.

Loading Conditions

  • Steady Pedaling
  • Startup Loading
  • Horizontal Loading
  • Vertical Loading
  • Rear Braking

Materials Compared

  • Structural Steel
  • Aluminum 6060-T4
  • Titanium Alloy
  • Carbon Fiber

Engineering Outcome

Identified governing loading case, compared stress and deformation across materials, and evaluated strength-to-weight tradeoffs.
