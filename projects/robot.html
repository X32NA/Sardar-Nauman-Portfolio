6-DOF Robotic Manipulator Design

Designed and modeled a six-degree-of-freedom robotic manipulator for articulated motion and workspace flexibility. The project focused on kinematic configuration, joint arrangement, and mechanical assembly design.

System Overview

  • 6 Rotational Degrees of Freedom
  • Articulated Joint Mechanism
  • Base, Shoulder, Elbow, Wrist Configuration
  • Full 3D Assembly Modeling

Robot Motion Demonstration

Engineering Highlights

  • Joint rotation range validation
  • Workspace evaluation
  • Mechanical interference checking
  • Component alignment optimization

Design Considerations

  • Structural rigidity of links
  • Joint load distribution
  • Compact actuator placement
  • Manufacturability and assembly
