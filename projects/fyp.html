Final Year Project: Thermal-Electric Analysis and Heat Exchanger Design for Electromagnetic Propulsion Platform

Project Overview

Designed a structural platform and integrated heat exchanger system for an electromagnetic propulsion device. The study focused on thermal-electric coupling and heat dissipation performance.

Engineering Objectives

  • Design mechanical support structure for propulsion device
  • Integrate custom heat exchanger for thermal regulation
  • Perform thermal-electric multiphysics simulation
  • Evaluate directional heat flux and temperature distribution

Simulation & Analysis

  • Thermal-Electric coupled analysis
  • Directional heat flux evaluation (W/m²)
  • Inner wall temperature contour mapping
  • Assessment of heat dissipation efficiency

Key Findings

  • Non-uniform heat flux observed along channel length
  • Maximum heat flux magnitude identified near coil region
  • Temperature gradient confirmed effective heat removal
  • Heat exchanger improved thermal stability of propulsion system
