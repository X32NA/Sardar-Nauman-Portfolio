Final Year Project: Electromagnetic Propulsion Platform with Integrated Heat Exchanger

Project Overview

Designed and developed a mechanical platform for an electromagnetic propulsion device with integrated thermal management using a custom heat exchanger system.

Engineering Objectives

  • Design structural platform for propulsion device mounting
  • Develop heat exchanger for thermal control
  • Ensure structural stability under operational loads
  • Optimize cooling performance for electromagnetic components

Design Components

  • Electromagnetic propulsion housing
  • Custom heat exchanger design
  • Cooling flow pathway
  • Mechanical support frame

Engineering Considerations

  • Heat dissipation from electromagnetic coils
  • Material selection for thermal conductivity
  • Structural rigidity of platform
  • Manufacturability and assembly

Skills Demonstrated

  • Mechanical system integration
  • Thermal management design
  • CAD modeling and assembly
  • Engineering problem-solving
