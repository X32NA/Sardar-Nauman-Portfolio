Transient PCM Thermal Energy Storage Analysis

Objective

To analyze transient melting behavior of Phase Change Material (PCM) integrated with a finned heat source using ANSYS Fluent.

Simulation Setup

  • 2D Pressure-based solver
  • Laminar flow model
  • Enthalpy-porosity method
  • Time step: 0.1 s
  • Total simulation time: 1200 s

Key Results

  • Melting initiated at approximately 4.6 seconds
  • Liquid fraction increased gradually over time
  • Latent heat absorption stabilized temperature rise
  • Fins enhanced conductive heat transfer
