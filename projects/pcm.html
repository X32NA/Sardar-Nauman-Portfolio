Transient PCM Thermal Energy Storage Analysis

Performed transient CFD simulation using ANSYS Fluent to model phase change behavior of a Phase Change Material (PCM). The enthalpy-porosity method was used to capture melting dynamics.

Liquid Fraction Contour

The liquid fraction contour illustrates the progression of melting within the PCM domain. Melting initiated near the heated boundary and propagated inward over time.

Temperature Distribution Contour

Temperature gradients confirm latent heat absorption during phase transition. The temperature plateau behavior is consistent with phase change characteristics.

Liquid Fraction vs Time

The time evolution of liquid fraction shows the transient melting rate and overall phase transition duration.

Temperature vs Time

Temperature variation over time demonstrates energy absorption and stabilization during the melting process.

Engineering Insights

  • Transient phase change successfully captured using enthalpy method.
  • Heat transfer governed by conduction during early stages.
  • Latent heat effect caused temperature stabilization during melting.
  • Simulation results validated expected PCM thermal behavior.
